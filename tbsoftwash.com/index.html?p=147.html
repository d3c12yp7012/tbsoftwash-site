FAQ's

Frequently asked questions about soft wash roof cleaning.

What are the black stains on my roof?

Gloeocapsa magma is a photosynthetic cyanobacteria consisting of a small group of algae spores. It is responsible for the black/dark green stains that form on roofs. These black stains are the bacteria themselves in mass amounts covering the surface of the roof shingles. Besides creating uninviting black streaks, these algae also feed on the limestone filler in your shingles causing harmful damage and deterioration to your roof.

What about my landscaping, are you going to kill it?

No, we properly prepare the landscaping surrounding your home. We generously water and then shield your plants if necessary. All gutter drains are redirected or bagged to catch any runoff that may occur. We take every precaution we can to protect your landscaping around the home.

Will you be using high or low pressure methods?

High pressure methods should never be used on any part of your roof. Therefore, we use a non-pressure soft wash method recommended by the roof covering manufacturers themselves.

What Is Soft Washing?

Soft washing is a cleaning method using low pressure and specialized solutions to safely remove mildew, bacteria, algae and other organic stains from roofs and other building exteriors. It is so named to differentiate the method from power washing.

Why should I clean my roof?

1. Health advantages– Mold, mildew, fungus and algae are all on the top 10 Allergy Irritants list. Mold and fungus are also known to cause Legionnaires Disease.

2. Prevent expensive roof replacement – Save your shingles and your money by taking care of your roof and preventing deterioration.

3. Extend the life of the roof – Protect your investment, Our biodegradable formula increases shingle life by up to 50%.

4. Improve the look of your home –Cleaning your roof is a small cost and makes a large difference!

Why is it such a problem now more than ever?

The roofing material industry has changed. In the last few decades roof covering manufacturers have changed the makeup and construction of the shingles. Newer roofing materials have less petroleum products in them, more materials (like fiberglass) that retain moisture as they age, and more limestone (organic material) inside of them which the plants just love to feed on!

Should I Wait to Have My Roof Cleaned?

If you have black streaks (black algae), moss, or lichen growing on your roof. It’s not just an eye sore, your roof is being damaged!

The organisms are feeding off the materials in your roof covering and wearing away at the integrity of your roof. Our goal is to completely eliminate these problems and keep your roof healthy for years to come.

How Long Do I Have To Wait To See Results?

You will start to see results the same day. Most of the black algae will be gone right away. However, the moss, lichen, and problem spots may take a few rain and sun cycles to completely wash off once and for all.
